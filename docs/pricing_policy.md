# Pricing Policy

## Software Products
- Software products are priced per license.
- Discounts are available only to existing customers during approved promotional periods.
- Typical discounts range between 5% and 15%.

## Hardware Products
- Hardware pricing is fixed and margin-sensitive.
- Discounts require manager approval.
- Excessive discounting may negatively impact revenue.

## Services
- Services are billed per engagement.
- Discounts are not standard and require finance approval.
